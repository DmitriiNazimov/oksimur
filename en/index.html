about me

Hello everybody, I'm Oksana Muratova. And I am a mystical artist who creates enchanted surrealism.

Since 2008, she has worked professionally in the “Art Doll” medium, and has diplomas and awards of international significance.

Therefore, my works have a deep trace of puppetry on the one hand, and attempts to introduce elements of junk art stylistics, on the other. I actively use sculpting on canvas, embroidery, as well as paper, fabrics and various found artifacts.

My images refer to fairy tales and fantasy worlds with surreal motifs and fictional characters.

My works are represented by galleries in Moscow, St. Petersburg and Sochi (Russia). I actively take part in international Russian and European exhibitions and was awarded diplomas as a winner of thematic competitions.

My works

To create my paintings I use different materials: fabric, paper, texture paste, embroidery, as well as various artifacts.Found children's toys, vintage jewelry, stones - these and other art objects in my works acquire a new status of museum exhibits. In this way, I promote conscious consumption, celebrating upcycling as the aesthetics of the future.
